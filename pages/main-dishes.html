Risotto with Pancetta and Pees

A creamy and comforting Italian classic, this risotto begins with rice grains toasted with butter, then deglazed with white wine for added depth. The rice is then slowly cooked using flavorful vegetable stock. The tender grains of rice are complemented by crispy bits of bacon and sweet green peas, creating a harmonious blend of textures and tastes.
